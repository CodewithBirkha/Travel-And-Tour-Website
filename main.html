Malikarjun Temple

Situated 46 kilometers from Khalanga in Malikarjun VDC, the Malikarjun Temple[1] contains idols of Lord Shiva and Goddess Parvati.[citation needed]Malika is workshipped as Parbati while Arjun represents Lord Shiva. Devotees throng this temple every year during the months of Ashar and Karthik by Nepali calendar. Gaura Festival is also celebrated here with highly population of darchula in this temple. This temple is also famous for a journey which is done by walking a long and ending it in this temple.[2] It is very famous in Darchula district.[3].

commanly known as Dodhara Chandani Bridge is a suspansion bridge over Mahakali(sharada) River with a length about 1,496.5m. Which is located on Kanchanpur District of Malakali Zone in Farwestern development Region. It is famous for it's unique style of engineering technique.There are only 2 vdc of nepal beyond mahakali river chadani and dodhara,connection with own land is by this bridge. Mahakali river also serves as the border between Nepal and India. It is near the shuklaphanta Wildlife Reserve is the one of the main attraction of this western Nepal. Bedkot

Daijee (English: Daijee) is a Bedkot Municipality in Kanchanpur District in Sudurpashchim Pradesh of south-western Nepal. At the time of the 1991 Nepal census it had a population of 6597 people living in 2088 individual households.[1] Bedkot lake is one of the most famous destination and pilligrimage for Hindu religion. Bedkot lake is situated 6 km north of Daijee.[2] One more tourist attraction is Linga, people love to visit for picnic as well as for Shiva Darshan (Hindu's God). Linga is very close to Daijee Chauraha (about 5 km), on the way to Bedkot Lake. Besides this, Daijee is growing as a junction of connecting peoples from Bhitrimadesh (Dadeldhura) and Tarai belt.
